Requirements for our datasets.

These are the requirements followed by our datasets.


R1. Data is in one of the selected smart city domains.

Our datasets are collected from Madrid's city hall open data web. The topic is on cultural activities and events on Madrid. Below you can find the links of the datasets:
Salas de espectáculos artísticos: teatros, cines, filmotecas, auditorios y salas de conciertos
Museos de la ciudad de Madrid
Actividades Culturales y de Ocio Municipal en los próximos 100 días

R2. Data is available as a CSV file.

The datasets are available in other extensions such as .xml, GEO or .rdf but the one we will be using is the CSV format.

R3. Data has an open license so it can be published.

The general conditions allow the reuse of documents for commercial and non-commercial means. More information is available at Datos Abiertos > Condiciones de uso.

R4. Data can be easily linked with generic real-world entities (e.g., locations).

The fields of the datasets can be linked with generic real-world entities such as museums and metro stations in Madrid.

R5. Documentation exists for the data.

In some of the datasets you can find a pdf document called Estructura de fichero.

R6. Data can come from multiple data sources.

We have found several linked datasets to find the relation between the cultural activity and its location in Madrid. It is possible that new sources would be needed, for example wikidata.
