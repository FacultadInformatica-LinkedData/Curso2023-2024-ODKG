  • Analyse Licensing of the Data Source

The publisher of both of the used datasets is the City Council of Madrid and the rightsholder is the “Dirección General de Planificación e Infraestructuras de Movilidad”.

The data has an open license that allows to reuse it for commercial and non-commercial purposes, as long as the following conditions are met:

  1. The sense of the information is not altered.
  2. The source of the information is cited.
  3. The date of the last update of the information is mentioned, as long as it was included in the original document.
  4. It is not insinuated that the City Council of Madrid supports or participates in the reutilization of the data.
  5. The metadata about the update date and the conditions for reuse are not altered or deleted.
  6. In the case of anonymized data for personal data protection purposes, the concerning people are not re-identified by using other sources.

This license can be found here: https://datos.madrid.es/egob/catalogo/aviso-legal

The license we should use for the dataset generated should be of the same kind.

  • Define Resource Naming Strategy

URI form: slash

URI Domain: http://grupo12_open_data.org/

Ontology path: http://grupo12_open_data.org/ontology/group12

  • Classes: http://grupo12_open_data.org/ontology/group12#[className]
  • Properties: http://grupo12_open_data.org/ontology/group12#[propertyName]
  • Individuals: http://grupo12_open_data.org/resource/[className]/[identifier]

Examples:

  • Classes: http://grupo12_open_data.org/ontology/group12#ParkingSpace
  • Properties: http://grupo12_open_data.org/ontology/group12#hasOccupancy
  • Individuals: http://grupo12_open_data.org/resource/codeDistrict/7
