Application requirements

The application allows users to see the common and current occupation of parking spaces in the city of Madrid. In other words, users of the application will be able to locate near municipal parking lots in a map, and see how full they normally are and if there is currently free spaces. Additionally, it will also show the prices and availability of municipal parking lots for residents (PAR).

The application will contain a map feature were the parking lots will be shown, as well as a search engine to locate specific parking spaces or city districts. By selecting a parking lot with either of these methods; its characteristics, occupation state and price will be shown, when available. 

This application will require data about:

  • Current and historic occupation in municipal parking lots in Madrid
  • Prices of parking spaces in municipal parking lots for residents in Madrid
