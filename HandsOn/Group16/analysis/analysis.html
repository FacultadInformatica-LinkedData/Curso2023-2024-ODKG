Datasets

The topic of the selected datasets are describe next:

  • desfibriladores_externos_fuera_ambito_sanitario.csv: Municipal and subsidized day centers in the city of Madrid specialized in elderly care.

Exploratory Data Analysis

From this analysis we can extract some conclusions as expressed next:

  • The address of the building where the defibrillator is located is separated into several columns. Also, the format of the addresses changes from row to row.
  • There are many null values due to the format used during data acquisition or missing data.
  • The format of the opening hours of the buildings shows a lot of variability in its format, as well as erroneous or null values.
