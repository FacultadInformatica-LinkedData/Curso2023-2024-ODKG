Group 15 - Application Requirements

The Electric Vehicle Charging Station Locator app will be designed to help people in Rostock find charging stations for their electric cars effortlessly. This user-friendly app will offer details like station locations, operator info, costs, and more, simplifying the charging process.

It's a handy tool for locating nearby stations, cutting charging costs, and supporting a cleaner environment. Plus, the dataset is regularly updated, ensuring users have the latest information for their electric vehicles in Rostock
