Â The application is for visitors of the Tate Museum, which is based in London, UK.

The main goal of the app is to provide informations about the art works during visits such as the name of the artist and other intersting facts.

In the app there will be pictures of the art works, pictures of the artists, their birth date, biography, context of the art piece...

One will be able to select the art work they are interested in and ask for more informations. There will be a research bar to type the name of each art work.
