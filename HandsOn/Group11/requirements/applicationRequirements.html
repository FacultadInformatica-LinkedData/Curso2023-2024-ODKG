Application Requirements

The app is dedicated for tourists in London, who wish to spend their time in an interesting way. The gathered datasets contain information about the places in London that different types of events may take place. In the app there will be map, with the search bar, where they can find and look for different locations, where occurrence take place in a specific moment. What is more, the additional functionality is that they can create a ¨to do¨ list with places selected by them and visible in the app. They can also save the places that they have checked earlier, as well as tag them on the map. The app is meant to enhance the experiance of trip for the users and allow local events and venues to become better known among visitors.
