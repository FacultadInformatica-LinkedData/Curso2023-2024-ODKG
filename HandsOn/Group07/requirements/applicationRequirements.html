Electric Vehicle Charging Stations App

Objective: To assist electric vehicle users in finding and accessing nearby charging stations.

App Features

  1. Search by City: Users can search for charging stations in a specific city.
  2. Search by Station: Users can get address and city of a specific station
  3. Station Details: Users can see additional informations of a specific station.
  4. Available Chargers: Users can see the number of available chargers for each type in a specific station (Level 1, Level 2, DC Fast, etc.).
  5. Opening Hours: Users can see opening hours of a specific station.
  6. Geographical Coordinates: Users can see latitude and longitude of a specific station.
