Electric Vehicle Charging Stations App

Objective: To assist electric vehicle users in finding and accessing nearby charging stations.

App Features

  1. Nearby Charging Stations: Using the user's current location, the app will display a list of nearby charging stations, sorted by distance. The list will also show the number of available charging points, operating hours, and other relevant information.
  2. Map View: Users can view charging stations on an interactive map.
  3. Search by City: Users can search for charging stations in a specific city.
  4. Filter Options: Users can filter the list based on the type of charger (Level 1, Level 2, DC Fast, etc.), operating hours, and other criteria.
  5. Station Details: Tapping on a station will display more detailed information, including the street address, operating hours, and the number of available charging points.
  6. User Reviews & Ratings: Users can leave reviews and ratings for each charging station, helping others make informed decisions.
  7. Favorites: Users can save frequently visited stations to their favorites for easy access.
  8. Route Planning: Users can plan their routes based on charging station availability, ensuring they don't run out of battery during their journey.
  9. Integration with Navigation Apps: Users can open directions to a selected charging station in their preferred navigation app.
