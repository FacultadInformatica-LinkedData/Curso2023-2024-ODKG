    Define Resource Naming Strategy.

    The URI domain chosen is http://http://healthcentersmadrid.es/. We selected this URI after checking that it was free.

    Path: http://healthcentersmadrid.es/locations

    Data: http://healthcentersmadrid.es/locations/resource

    Ontology: http://healthcentersmadrid.es/locations/ontology

    • Classes:
      - http://healthcentersmadrid.es/locations/ontology/<ontologyName>#<className>
    • Individuals:
      - http://healthcentersmadrid.es/locations/resource/<className>/<identifier> .
    • Properties:
      - http://healthcentersmadrid.es/locations/ontology/<ontologyName>#<propertyName> .
